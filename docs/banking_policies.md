# VietBank Policies and Regulations

## Policy 1: Know Your Customer (KYC)

### 1.1 Customer Identification
All customers must undergo KYC verification:
- Vietnamese citizens: CCCD (12-digit Citizen Identity Card) is mandatory
- Foreign nationals: Passport with valid visa or residence permit
- Legal entities: Business registration, tax certificate, authorized signatories

### 1.2 Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)
EDD is required for:
- Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs)
- High-value transactions exceeding 400,000,000 VND
- Customers from high-risk jurisdictions
- Complex ownership structures

### 1.3 Customer Information Update
Customers must update their information:
- Every 2 years for standard accounts
- Every year for high-risk accounts
- Immediately upon change of personal details (address, phone, etc.)

Failure to update may result in account restrictions until verification is completed.

## Policy 2: Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

### 2.1 Transaction Monitoring
VietBank monitors all transactions for suspicious activity:
- Cash transactions ≥ 400,000,000 VND require reporting to State Bank
- Multiple transactions appearing to split a larger amount (structuring)
- Transactions inconsistent with customer profile
- Rapid movement of funds through multiple accounts

### 2.2 Suspicious Transaction Reporting
- Staff must file internal Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) within 24 hours
- Compliance department reviews within 3 business days
- Report to State Bank of Vietnam within 5 business days if confirmed
- Customer must NOT be informed of the report (tipping-off prohibition)

### 2.3 Record Keeping
- Transaction records retained for minimum 5 years
- Customer identification records retained for 5 years after account closure
- SAR records retained indefinitely

## Policy 3: Data Privacy and Protection

### 3.1 Personal Data Collection
VietBank collects and processes personal data including:
- Full name, date of birth, CCCD number
- Address, phone number, email
- Employment and income information
- Transaction history and account balances
- Biometric data (fingerprint, facial recognition) for app authentication

### 3.2 Data Usage
Personal data is used for:
- Account management and service delivery
- Credit assessment and risk management
- Regulatory compliance and reporting
- Marketing (with customer consent only)
- Fraud prevention and security

### 3.3 Data Sharing
Customer data may be shared with:
- State Bank of Vietnam (regulatory requirement)
- Vietnam Asset Management Company (VAMC) for NPL management
- Credit Information Center (CIC) for credit reporting
- Law enforcement (with valid legal order)
- Partner insurers (with customer consent)

Data is NEVER shared with unauthorized third parties or sold to data brokers.

### 3.4 Customer Rights
Under Vietnamese data protection law:
- Right to access personal data held by VietBank
- Right to correct inaccurate data
- Right to request deletion (subject to regulatory retention requirements)
- Right to withdraw consent for marketing
- Right to lodge complaint with data protection authority

## Policy 4: Interest Rate and Fee Policy

### 4.1 Interest Rate Determination
Lending rates are based on:
- State Bank of Vietnam operating rate
- VietBank cost of funds
- Customer risk profile (credit grade A-D)
- Loan purpose and collateral value
- Market competition

Deposit rates follow State Bank ceilings:
- Demand deposits: max 1.0% per annum
- Term deposits < 6 months: max 4.75% per annum
- Term deposits ≥ 6 months: market-determined

### 4.2 Fee Schedule Changes
- Fees may be adjusted quarterly
- Customers notified 30 days before any fee increase
- New fees posted on website, branches, and mobile app
- Existing customers grandfathered for 90 days on fee increases

## Policy 5: Loan Collection and Default

### 5.1 Overdue Classification
| Days Past Due | Classification | Provision Rate |
|---------------|---------------|----------------|
| 0-10 | Current | 0% |
| 10-30 | Special Mention | 5% |
| 31-90 | Substandard | 20% |
| 91-180 | Doubtful | 50% |
| 181+ | Loss | 100% |

### 5.2 Collection Process
1. **Day 1-10**: Automated SMS/email reminder
2. **Day 11-30**: Phone call from relationship manager
3. **Day 31-60**: Formal demand letter sent
4. **Day 61-90**: Collection team engagement, restructuring offered
5. **Day 91+**: Legal proceedings may commence
6. **Day 181+**: Collateral foreclosure or write-off consideration

### 5.3 Loan Restructuring
Eligible customers may request restructuring:
- Extension of loan term (up to 50% of original term)
- Interest rate adjustment
- Grace period for principal payment (up to 6 months)
- Partial debt forgiveness (exceptional cases, requires board approval)

## Policy 6: Digital Security

### 6.1 Authentication Standards
- Passwords: minimum 8 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, number, special character
- Password change required every 90 days
- Account lockout after 5 failed attempts
- OTP validity: 3 minutes
- Session timeout: 5 minutes of inactivity

### 6.2 Transaction Security
- All transactions encrypted with TLS 1.3
- Domestic transfers > 50,000,000 VND require OTP
- International transfers require dual authorization
- Card-not-present transactions require 3D Secure
- Real-time fraud scoring on every transaction

### 6.3 Incident Response
In case of security breach:
1. Immediate containment and investigation
2. Customer notification within 72 hours
3. State Bank notification within 24 hours
4. Remediation measures within 30 days
5. Post-incident review and policy update

## Policy 7: Cross-Border Transactions

### 7.1 Foreign Exchange
- Exchange rates updated every 30 minutes during business hours
- Rate spread: ±0.5% for amounts < 10,000 USD, negotiable for larger amounts
- Forward contracts available for commercial customers
- Maximum individual remittance: 50,000 USD per transaction

### 7.2 International Transfer
- SWIFT transfers: 1-3 business days
- Correspondent banks: JP Morgan, Citibank, HSBC, Standard Chartered
- Required information: SWIFT/BIC code, beneficiary account, purpose of payment
- Foreign exchange control: All transfers must comply with State Bank regulations

### 7.3 Trade Finance
- Letters of Credit (L/C): Issuance fee 0.15-0.25% of value
- Documentary collections: Fee 0.1% of value
- Bank guarantees: Fee 1-2% per annum of guaranteed amount
- Trade loans: Preferential rates for exporters
