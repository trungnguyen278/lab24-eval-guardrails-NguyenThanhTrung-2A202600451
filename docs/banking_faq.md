# Banking FAQ - VietBank Digital Services

## Chapter 1: Account Opening

### 1.1 Personal Account Requirements
To open a personal savings account at VietBank, customers must provide:
- Valid Citizen Identity Card (CCCD) with 12-digit number
- Proof of residential address (utility bill or rental contract)
- Minimum initial deposit of 500,000 VND
- Tax identification number (TIN) for interest reporting

The account opening process typically takes 15-30 minutes at any VietBank branch. Online account opening is available through the VietBank Mobile app for customers aged 18 and above.

### 1.2 Business Account Requirements
Business accounts require additional documentation:
- Business registration certificate (Giấy chứng nhận đăng ký kinh doanh)
- Company seal specimen
- Board resolution authorizing account opening
- Tax registration certificate
- Minimum deposit of 10,000,000 VND

### 1.3 Account Types
VietBank offers several account types:
1. **Standard Savings** - 4.5% annual interest, no minimum balance after opening
2. **Premium Savings** - 5.8% annual interest, minimum balance 100,000,000 VND
3. **Term Deposit** - 6.2-7.5% depending on term (3, 6, 12, 24 months)
4. **Current Account** - 0.1% interest, unlimited transactions
5. **Student Account** - 3.0% interest, no fees for students under 25

## Chapter 2: Loan Products

### 2.1 Personal Loans
Personal loan offerings include:
- **Consumer Loan**: Up to 500,000,000 VND, 8.5-12% interest rate, term 12-60 months
- **Home Loan**: Up to 5,000,000,000 VND, 7.5-9% interest rate, term up to 25 years
- **Auto Loan**: Up to 2,000,000,000 VND, 7.0-8.5% interest rate, term 3-7 years
- **Education Loan**: Up to 200,000,000 VND, 6.5% preferential rate

### 2.2 Loan Application Process
Step-by-step process:
1. Submit application form with required documents
2. Credit assessment (3-5 business days)
3. Collateral valuation if applicable
4. Loan committee approval
5. Contract signing and disbursement

Required documents for personal loans:
- CCCD and household registration book
- Income proof (salary slips for 3 months or tax declaration)
- Collateral documents (for secured loans)
- Purpose of loan documentation

### 2.3 Interest Rate Policy
Interest rates are determined by:
- Vietnam State Bank base rate (currently 4.5%)
- Customer credit score (VietBank internal scoring A-D)
- Loan-to-value ratio
- Relationship history with VietBank
- Market conditions and interbank rates

Customers with Grade A credit (score > 750) receive preferential rates 0.5-1.0% below standard.

Features available on the mobile app:

- QR code payments
- Account management and settings
- Loan application and tracking

### 4.2 Security Features
VietBank employs multiple security layers:
1. **Biometric authentication**: Fingerprint and Face ID
2. **Two-factor authentication (2FA)**: OTP via SMS or authenticator app
3. **Transaction limits**: Customizable daily/monthly limits
4. **Device binding**: Each account linked to max 2 devices
5. **Real-time fraud detection**: AI-powered transaction monitoring
6. **Session timeout**: Auto-logout after 5 minutes of inactivity
